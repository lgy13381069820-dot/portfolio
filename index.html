Airport Fire Equipment Delivery Optimization

Sep 2024 – Nov 2024 Individual
Problem

The company experienced significant variation in delivery and installation cycles across multiple airport projects (Shenzhen, Guangzhou, Nanchang, Xi'an), with some delays. Need to analyze historical project data, identify key factors affecting delivery time, and develop more accurate bidding and production scheduling strategies.

Data

Extended dataset based on real work experience (anonymized), containing 15 airport project records with fields: project value, client type, installation season, German factory lead time, contractor cooperation score, final delivery delay (days).

Approach

Cleaned data with Python (pandas), handled missing values, created binary label "delay > 10 days". Used decision tree to identify key drivers ("installation season + contractor cooperation score" as the most important split). Plotted feature importance bar chart and delay heatmap with matplotlib.

Outcome

Proposed "book contractor schedule 2 weeks earlier for winter projects" — estimated 30% reduction in delay risk. Created a one-page "pre-bid checklist" for account managers based on model rules.

My Contribution

Completed all data processing, modeling, and business interpretation independently.

Market Data Analysis & Strategy for a Startup

Apr 2023 – Jun 2023 Team · 4 members
Problem

An environmental tech startup wanted to enter the North China industrial fire protection market but lacked understanding of customer purchasing behavior and price sensitivity. The team needed to analyze public tender data, map the market landscape, and provide pricing & promotion recommendations.

Data

Scraped from Chinese government procurement websites, covering 2019–2022 North China industrial fire tenders. 1,200 records including winning bid price, number of bidders, buyer type, evaluation method.

Approach

Applied K-means clustering to segment buyers into three types: price-sensitive, technology-preferring, brand-loyal. Used box plots for price tolerance analysis per segment. Built a simple linear regression to test "number of bidders → price reduction magnitude".

Outcome

Submitted a 10-page analysis report to the founder, recommending differentiated technical solutions for "technology-preferring" buyers. The startup adjusted its sales scripts and pricing templates accordingly; 2 out of 3 subsequent bids reached final negotiation.

My Contribution

Built the clustering model, price tolerance charts, and wrote the "strategic recommendations" section.
